Theatre (version2)

This is a different version of my "Theatre" painting. I wasn't fully satisfied with the first version, so I revisited it to give it a focus point and make it more dramatic. The contrast between light and shadow is more pronounced now, giving the piece a stronger theatrical feel.

I might decide to make a third version in the future, experimenting further with lighting and composition.

Theatre (version1)

This painting was my first attempt at capturing the dramatic lighting of a theatre scene. While I liked the overall composition, I felt it lacked a strong focal point and the lighting could be more dynamic.

I mainly like that i just decided to go in head first without thinking too much about it. This gave me an opotunity to be bold with my brush strokes and color choices.

This version served as a learning experience for me, and I used what I learned here to create a more refined second version.

Interrupted (Updated)

This is an updated version of "Interrupted". After selling the first one, I felt like it didn't fully capture what I wanted to express. So, I revisited the piece, adding more details and refining the colors to better convey the emotions behind it. This version feels a little closer to being complete.
